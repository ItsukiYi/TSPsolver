ENGLISH VERSION

[0:00-0:15] HOOK — What's the problem?
─────────────────────────────────────
Hi, I'm Pan Qiao from Group 36. My project asks a simple question:
can we solve the Traveling Salesman Problem better by combining
classical algorithms with modern neural networks, rather than
choosing between them?

TSP is NP-hard. It's everywhere — food delivery, warehouse routing,
chip design. Classical algorithms like Christofides give provable
guarantees. But since 2023, diffusion models and reinforcement
learning have entered the arena. The question is: do they compete,
or do they complement?

[0:15-0:50] WHAT WE DID — Six methods, one pipeline
────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I implemented four classical algorithms from scratch — Nearest
Neighbor, Christofides, 2-opt, and LKH3. I also reproduced two
state-of-the-art neural solvers: DIFUSCO from NeurIPS 2023, which
uses diffusion models to generate TSP tours, and DualOpt from AAAI
2025, which uses reinforcement learning to refine them. All of this
on a single RTX 2060 — the original papers used eight GPUs.

The core contribution — and the centerpiece of this poster — is
the DIFUSCO-to-DualOpt hybrid pipeline. Here's the key insight:
DIFUSCO's diffusion process captures GLOBAL tour structure — it sees
the entire graph at once. But its output has local defects like
self-crossings. DualOpt's revisers excel at LOCAL refinement — they
fix exactly those defects. Neither is sufficient alone. Together,
they achieve results that neither can reach independently.

[0:50-1:20] KEY RESULTS — The numbers that matter
──────────────────────────────────────────────────
On TSP-50: our pipeline achieves minus 1.67 percent versus ground
truth — the BEST result of any method we tested, and the only one
that consistently beats the training labels. The DualOpt reviser
extracts 24 percent more improvement than standard 2-opt from the
same heatmap.

On TSPLIB benchmarks: DIFUSCO, trained only on TSP-50, generalizes
to TSP-1002 — that's twenty times larger — with just a 7 percent gap.
And on kroA100, our pipeline actually surpasses the original published
DualOpt result. We also tested on a custom clustered delivery dataset
that neither original paper evaluated on.

[1:20-1:45] WHAT WE LEARNED — Five improvements, one breakthrough
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I tried five strategies to integrate the heatmap with the reviser.
Four failed. But understanding WHY they failed taught us something
fundamental: TSP-50 is essentially "solved" for DualOpt's reviser —
no perturbation escapes its local optimum. The only intervention
that works is changing the INITIAL TOUR entirely, not trying to
guide the reviser mid-flight.

[1:45-2:00] TAKEAWAY
─────────────────────
Three things to remember. First: classical methods still win at
small scales — LKH3 achieves a 0.4 percent gap in 1.2 seconds.
Second: neural methods are designed for large scales — DualOpt
beats LKH3 by 1.4 percent on TSP-100K. Third: the most promising
direction is not classical OR neural — it is classical AND neural.
Thank you!

[0:00-0:15] 开场 — 问题是什么？
───────────────────────────────
大家好，我是Group 36的Pan Qiao。我的项目回答一个简单的问题：
旅行商问题——TSP——能不能通过结合经典算法和现代神经网络来解得更好，
而不是在两者之间选边？

TSP是NP-hard的，但无处不在——外卖配送、仓库路径规划、芯片设计。
经典算法如Christofides能给出可证明的1.5倍近似保证。但从2023年开始，
扩散模型和强化学习也进入了这个领域。问题是：它们是竞争关系，还是互补关系？

[0:15-0:50] 我们做了什么 —— 六种方法，一个pipeline
────────────────────────────────────────────────
我从零实现了四种经典算法——最近邻、Christofides、2-opt、LKH3。
同时复现了两个SOTA神经求解器：DIFUSCO（NeurIPS 2023，用扩散模型生成TSP tour）
和DualOpt（AAAI 2025，用强化学习精炼tour）。全部跑在一张RTX 2060上——
原论文用的是8张GPU。

核心贡献——也是这张海报的视觉中心——是DIFUSCO到DualOpt的混合pipeline。
关键洞察是：DIFUSCO的扩散过程捕捉全局tour结构，但输出有局部缺陷（自交叉）。
DualOpt的reviser擅长局部精炼——恰好修复这些缺陷。单独用谁都不够好，
合在一起，达到了任何单一方法都无法达到的效果。

[0:50-1:20] 关键结果 —— 几个重要的数字
───────────────────────────────────────
TSP-50上：我们的pipeline达到-1.67% vs ground truth——所有测试方法中最好，
也是唯一一个稳定超越训练标签的。DualOpt的reviser从同一个heatmap中
比标准2-opt多提取了24%的改进。

TSPLIB基准上：DIFUSCO仅用TSP-50训练，泛化到TSP-1002（大20倍），
只有7%的gap。在kroA100上，我们的pipeline超越了原版DualOpt的发表结果。
我们还在自己设计的clustered delivery数据集上做了测试——
这是两篇原论文都没评估过的场景。

[1:20-1:45] 我们学到了什么 —— 五个改进，一个突破
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
我尝试了五种策略来整合heatmap和reviser。四种失败了。但理解它们
为什么失败，教会了我们一些根本性的东西：TSP-50对DualOpt的reviser来说
已经是"solved"状态——任何扰动都无法逃离它的局部最优。唯一有效的干预
是彻底替换初始tour，而不是试图在半路引导reviser。

[1:45-2:00] 总结
─────────────────
记住三点。第一：小规模下经典方法依然胜出——LKH3用1.2秒达到0.4%的gap。
第二：神经方法是为大规模设计的——DualOpt在TSP-100K上比LKH3好1.4%且快104倍。
第三：最有前景的方向不是classical OR neural——而是classical AND neural。
谢谢！
